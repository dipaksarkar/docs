# General Settings

The General Settings module allows you to configure the foundational identity and behavior of your Mailzor application.

## Core Application Details

Configure the primary contact and branding information for your platform.

- **App Name**: The name of your service as it will appear in the browser title, email subjects, and throughout the interface.
- **Admin Email**: The primary contact email address for administrative notifications.
- **Phone Number**: (Optional) Your business contact number.
- **Timezone**: Set the default timezone for the application. This ensures all campaign schedules and activity logs are displayed in your local time.
- **Primary Language**: Choose the default interface language for all users.

## Localization & Currency

- **Default Country**: Select your primary operational region.
- **Default Currency**: Choose the currency for your subscription plans and orders (e.g., USD, EUR).
- **Currency Format**: Choose how prices are displayed across the platform:
  - `Symbol only`: e.g., $100
  - `Symbol + Code`: e.g., $100 USD
  - `Code only`: e.g., USD 100

## Branding

Upload your brand assets to personalize the Mailzor experience.

- **Site Logo**: The primary logo for light backgrounds.
- **Logo (Dark)**: A specialized version of your logo for dark-themed interfaces or dark mode users.

## Updating Settings

**Steps:**

1.  Navigate to **Settings > General** in your admin dashboard.
2.  Update the fields in the **General Settings** tab.
3.  Click **Save** to apply the changes globally.
