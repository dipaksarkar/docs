# Personalization & Appearance

Mailzor allows administrators to customize their individual dashboard experience without affecting other staff members or end-users.

## Appearance Settings

Tailor the look and feel of the admin interface to your preference.

- **Theme Mode**: Choose between:
  - **Light**: A clean, high-contrast light interface.
  - **Dark**: A sophisticated dark mode that reduces eye strain.
  - **System**: Automatically follows your operating system's theme settings.

## Regional Preferences

Configure how dates, times, and financial data are displayed for your specific admin account.

- **Timezone**: Select your local timezone. This overrides the global application timezone for your specific view of campaign schedules and logs.
- **Locale**: Choose your preferred interface language for the admin panel.
- **Default Currency**: Set the default currency you wish to see in reports and financial overviews.

## Updating Your Preferences

1.  Click on your **Profile Avatar** in the top-right corner of the header.
2.  Select **Appearance** (or **Personalization**).
3.  Adjust the theme, timezone, or locale to your liking.
4.  Click **Save**. The changes will be applied immediately.
